Just a guy reaching for the sky (not quite the stars)

An inside look in the life of Phil Cloutier

Biography:

I was born in montreal in 1995. I lived in the area for 7 years until I moved to missisauga with my mom and brother. After a couple years, we moved to Innisfil, Ontario, where I spent most of my formative years. I went to a French Catholic highschool where I met my best friends who i still keep in touch with daily, all thanks to modern techonology. (aka Discord and video games) After highschool, I did a semester of the electrical technician course but unfortunately dropped out as the dangers of it intimidated me. If I could go back, I would of kept with it as I would be much better off now financially. Although, they say forsight is 20/20.

After I dropped out, I worked at a factory for a few months and then did Stucco for homes for about a year. After that, I moved to Fort Mcmurray, Alberta, in search for a well-paying job. I was working in fast-food while I was waiting on a Union list. Before i was able to secure a good job, my cat and I had to evacuate for 3 months due to the forest fires that ate a good part of the neighbourhoods surrounding the City. We fell inlove with Vancouver Island and lived in Nanaimo for a bit over a year's time. (sadly, I didnt try the Nanaimo bars)

I moved back to Ontario and completed police foundations at Georgian College in Orillia. I then worked as a security guard at Georgian College for a year and a half. I realised that I did not want to pursue that career for several reasons and moved to Midland where I worked for Tay township and then the City of Midland in the Operations department. After seeing the success my friends were having in the tech industry, I decided to pursue an education in programming as I have always been passionate about computers and how they work. I've experienced many different fields of work such as Construction (siding, large-scale Framing), Kitchens, Customer service, city operations, landscaping, security, duct cleaning, and a few different types of factories. I am hoping that i find my place in the tech industry as it is a constantly evolving environement, contains problem solving, and is a part of of almost everything we encounter today.

It honestly blows my mind everytime i think that all this techonology that we take for granted came from ingenious men and women who put some metal and plastic together and told it to do things. I look forward to the many things you have to teach us this semester!
